PHILOSOPHY

01
UNDERSTAND DEEPLY

Before a single line of code, I invest in understanding the problem space, constraints, users, and business context. Good software starts with great questions.

Requirements evolve. I architect systems that are inherently adaptable — clean contracts, decoupled modules, and well-defined boundaries that make tomorrow's changes easy.
